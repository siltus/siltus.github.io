Uncomment | &#8984; + `/` | &#8984; + `/` | &#8984; + `/` | `CTRL` + `K` &rarr; `CTRL` + `U` |
Find "Anything" | &#8984; + `SHIFT` + `O` | `SHIFT` `SHIFT` | &#8984; + `P` | `CTRL` + `,`
Go to line | &#8984; + `L` | &#8984; + `L` | `CTRL` + `G` | `CTRL` + `G`

Navigate Forward | `CTRL` + &#8984; + &rarr; | &#8984; + `]` | `CTRL` + `SHIFT` + `-` | `CTRL` + `SHIFT` + `-`
